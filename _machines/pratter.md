* __Distributor:__ [Pratter](https://pratter.co.id/){:target="_blank"}, Indonesia
* __Machines:__ models with Autonics controls as well as PLC equipped machines
* __Connection:__ MODBUS RTU via USB; requires the installation of a [serial driver](/modbus_serial/)
* __Features:__ 
  - logging of environmental temperature (ET), bean temperature (BT) and related rate-of-rise curves
  - PLC equipped machines allow to log and control the burner level, fan and drum speed
