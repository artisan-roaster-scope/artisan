* __Producer:__ [Neuhaus Neotec](https://www.neuhaus-neotec.com/){:target="_blank"}, Germany
* __Machines:__ RFB Junior, RFB-S, Neoroast
* __Connection:__ MODBUS UDB/TCP via network
* __Features:__
  - logging of environmental temperature (ET), bean temperature (BT) and related rate-of-rise curves as well as fan speed



**Watch out!**
You need to indicate the correct IP address of your machine to Artisan. Default IP addresses of the machines are 192.168.111.99 (RFB -Junior) or 192.168.111.73 (RFB-S), 192.268.5.89 (Neoroast). Your machines IP address might differ.
