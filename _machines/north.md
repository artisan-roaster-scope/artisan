* __Distributor:__ North Roasters, China
* __Machines:__ with Fotek control panel
* __Connection:__ MODBUS RTU via USB
* __Features:__ logging of bean temperature (BT), environmental temperature (ET). Some machines deliver the drum temperature and air flow data as well.

{: .notice--primary}
**Watch out!** North Roasters has never supported the Artisan project like many of the manufacturers here.  For that reason, we are unable to offer support for your specific roaster, especially if it differs from the included one-click machine setups.  Please contact North Roasters support if you encounter setup issues.
{: .notice--primary}


* __Standard Control Panel (Fotek)__  
(500g to 3kg, 2020 – Present)  
`North >> Standard Control Panel (Fotek)`  
  - BT (bean temperature)
  - ET (environmental temperature)
  - DT (drum temperature, optional)
  - air flow (0-60 Hz, optional)
