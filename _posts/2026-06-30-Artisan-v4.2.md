# What is new?
{: style="color: #4C97C3;" }

## Connectivity
{: style="color: #4C97C3;" }

We worked together with the ROEST team to connect Artisan as well as our [artisan.plus](https://artisan.plus) platform to the [ROEST platform](https://www.roestcoffee.com/). Users of [ROEST](https://artisan-scope.org/machines/roest/) machines can log their roasts with Artisan and manage their inventory on artisan.plus. This works also for their new P3000 machine!

<a href="https://artisan-scope.org/machines/roest/">
<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/roest.jpg" width="60%"></a>
{: .text-center}

The communication with the ROEST platform is using the [MQTT protocol](https://artisan-scope.org/devices/mqtt/). Thus we added a generic MQTT layer to Artisan the very same way we did for MODBUS, Siemens S7, and WebSockets before.

<a href="https://artisan-scope.org/devices/mqtt/">
<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/mqtt-logo.webp" width="60%"></a>
{: .text-center}

We also added a tight integration with the OB-1 PRO from our machine sponsor [Orbiter](https://artisan-scope.org/machines/orbiter/). Check out this nice initial review of this brand-new 1.2kg machine.

{% include video id="Cor_Wx6-SsE" provider="youtube" %}

Further, we added support for machines of [Pratter](https://artisan-scope.org/machines/pratter/), [Neuhaus Neotec](https://artisan-scope.org/machines/neuhaus/), [North Roasters](https://artisan-scope.org/machines/north/), the refurbished machines of [Schuilenburg](https://artisan-scope.org/machines/schuilenburg/) as well as the latest generation of [Probats G/UG 2026 series](https://artisan-scope.org/machines/probat/) which got updated this year with new software communicating via WebSockets as their P series machines.


## Core
{: style="color: #4C97C3;" }

We hardened the core of Artisan by adding validation of profiles and settings on load. The community contributed Bulgarian localization (thanks, Alex!) and reported many small issues in previous versions, which we fixed and complemented with small UI improvements.


## RoastGuard
{: style="color: #4C97C3;" }

It is generally accepted that when multiple roasts of the same green lot achieve the same roasting parameters, they can be considered effectively interchangeable. 

[<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/posts/2026-06-26-RoastGuard_teaser.webp" width="40%">]({% post_url 2026-06-26-RoastGuard %})
{: .text-center}

Our newest [artisan.plus](https://artisan.plus){:target="_blank"} feature, [RoastGuard](https://artisan-scope.org/release/RoastGuard/){:target="_blank"}, takes this principle and turns it into a tool for managing quality control processes.

## Supported Platforms
{: style="color: #4C97C3;" }

The platforms supported by Artisan v4.2 are

- macOS 14 Sonoma and newer on ARM (Apple Silicon) 
- macOS 13 Ventura and newer on Intel (x86-64)
- Windows 10 and newer on Intel (x86-64)
- RedHat 8.6 and newer
- Ubuntu 24.04 and newer
- Linux glibc>=2.39
- Raspberry Pi OS 13 Trixie
